Xiaomi's chairman Lei Jun made a speech as the company went public

published at: 2018-07-09T20:35:00+08:00

The speech text is listed below:

Hello, ladies and gentlemen!

At this moment,right here,i am extremely excited!

Eight years ago, I had a crazy idea: to take advantage of the Internet to make mobile phones,which enhances the global image of Chinese products, and benefits people all over the world! To be honest, few people believe my crazy idea.Thankfully,the first day I started the company,13 people came over and we had rice porridge.So far I still have not known if they actually believed my idea.They are all here and you can ask them about it.Today,there are 175 Xiaomi fellows here who represent 19,000 employees of the company.Thank all the fellows and their families.Xiaomi would not be so successful without them.

Recently,there is a trade war between China and the US and the global capital markets are experiencing ups and downs.I would like to thank more than ten investors at this moment who take money from their pockets to support Xiaomi,including Li Kashing,Jack Ma and Pony Ma.Thank you! Despite the bad economic trends,good companies will always stand out!

I'd like to thank the fans of Xiaomi community all over the world in particular.Eight years ago,we shipped our first product MIUI. The first version of MIUI had only 100 users.It is with the support of those 100 users that we've come all the way step by step and now there are 190 million monthly active users on Xiaomi's platform.Today we invite six fans who have supported Xiaomi since the birth of Xiaomi in 2010 to come here.Later,I will invite Mr Hong Jun who represents those fans to join in the opening ceremony.All our efforts are for the recognition and satisfaction of Xiaomi fans.Our achievements would be impossible without their support.

We'd also like to thank the HKEX and the SFC.Xiaomi is an Internet company.From day one,we have applied the system of different rights of the same stock.Without innovation of Hong Kong's capital markets,it would be difficult for Xiaomi to list on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.I believe Hong Kong will see more quality Internet companies come here!

Since the first day of Xiaomi,innovation is in the DNA of Xiaomi.But real innovation is never easy.There is no innovation without great effort,no innovation without numerous setbacks,and no innovation without misunderstanding or even criticism.The more you understand this, the more grateful you become.Thank you for all the understanding,trust,encouragement and support!

It is not enough to just say gratitude words,the best way to express gratitude is to continue to work hard.We will strive to make good products and repay our fans all over the world.We will do well in the company and reward our employees and investors.The stock will start trading soon,which is a brand new start for Xiaomi!

Thank you!Thank you very much! ​​​
